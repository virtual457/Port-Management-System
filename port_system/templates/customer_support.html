Frequently Asked Questions (or "Common Sailor Confusions")

Why can't I find my cargo? Has it been lost at sea?

Unless you're shipping to the Bermuda Triangle, your cargo is likely just fine! Try refreshing the page, checking your filters, or looking under "My Bookings." If all else fails, perhaps the cargo has developed sentience and is taking a vacation. (Just kidding, contact support and we'll help you track it down.)

Why is shipping so expensive? Are you secretly funding a pirate fleet?

While keeping a pirate fleet would certainly be exciting, our prices are based on distance, fuel costs, crew salaries, and the occasional kraken insurance policy. Rest assured, we're constantly working to optimize routes and provide competitive rates. No buried treasure required!

My booking was marked "Delivered" but my cargo is still missing!

Double-check the delivery address - perhaps your cargo was delivered to Davy Jones' Locker instead? More likely, there might be a processing delay at the port. Contact your local port authority or our support team with your booking reference for assistance. We promise we didn't feed it to the sharks.

Can I ship my mother-in-law?

As tempting as it might be, we regret to inform you that human shipping is strictly prohibited by maritime law. Perhaps consider a nice cruise vacation for her instead? We hear the Titanic II is lovely this time of year. (For legal reasons, that's a joke.)

The system shows no available routes between my ports. What now?

Either you're trying to ship between alternative dimensions, or we simply don't have established routes between those specific ports yet. Consider nearby ports as alternatives, or contact our business development team to request a new route. If you're really determined, we suggest carrier pigeons (warning: not recommended for packages over 1 ounce).

I forgot my password. Has it been claimed by the sea?

Your password hasn't been swallowed by a whale - just click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you're still having trouble, our support team can help you regain access to your account. Just be prepared to answer security questions like "What was the name of your first pet goldfish?" and "If you were a sea creature, what would you be?"
